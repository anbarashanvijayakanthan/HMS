The patient's actual journey (this is the workflow that ties everything together):

Receptionist registers the patient (Patient Registration) → books an appointment (Appointment Management) → adds them to the queue (Queue Management) → later collects payment (Billing Collection)
Nurse picks them from the queue → records vitals (BP, temp, SpO2, etc.) → flags critical cases → marks patient "Ready for Doctor"
Doctor opens the case → sees vitals + history → adds Diagnosis → writes Prescription (→ sent to Pharmacy) → orders Lab Tests (→ sent to Lab) → orders Radiology → schedules Follow-up
Pharmacist receives the prescription in their queue → dispenses medicine → checks inventory/stock → bills the patient
Lab Technician receives the lab order → collects sample → enters results → generates report → sends back to doctor
Loop back to Receptionist for follow-up scheduling/billing, and the doctor sees lab results next time they open the case.
